About Me

Designing spaces that inspire and endure.

I'm Alex Carter, an emerging architectural designer with a passion for creating spaces that harmonize aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability. My approach combines rigorous technical knowledge with creative problem-solving to deliver designs that stand the test of time.

Currently completing my Master of Architecture at the University of Design Excellence, I've developed a distinctive design philosophy that values context, materiality, and human experience equally. My work has been recognized with multiple awards, including the prestigious AIA Student Honor Award.

Design Philosophy

I believe architecture should serve both practical needs and elevate the human spirit, creating spaces that are as functional as they are inspiring.

Technical Expertise

Proficient in BIM modeling, sustainable design principles, and construction documentation with a focus on detail-oriented execution.
